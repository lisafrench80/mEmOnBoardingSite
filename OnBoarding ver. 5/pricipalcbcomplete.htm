The Core Beliefs Profile provides us a glimpse into your school's mission, culture, and instructional model. Taking no more than 10 minutes to complete, we use this profile to determine how good of a “fit” an applicant is for your specific school. Principals are the only individuals at schools who can fill out the Core Beliefs Profile. This is because school leaders are integral to the culture of school, and study after study have shown that misalignment with the mission, vision, and culture set by the school’s administration is the primary driver of teacher attrition. Your specific answers to each question will be kept private and will not be shared with potential candidates, but our “Match Score” is calculated on the basis of your answers to these questions.

Step 3: The last step is to respond to questions about your school's mission, staff culture, professional development, instruction, planning, student culture, and school environment. Once you have completed this brief, 5-10 minute survey, you will be able to access our portal, view Culture Match Scores and use our arsenal of convenience features.

ExpertTip: Your specific answers to each question will be kept private, but will be used to calculate the overall Match score between your school and potential candidates. To help find the right talent and promote retention, be as honest as possible in your responses. Please note that you will *not* be able to view the Culture Match Scores of any potential candidates until the principal completes this profile.
