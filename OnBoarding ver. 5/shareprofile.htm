After you've worked hard to build your professional profile, don't keep it under wraps. Share it on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and in emails - because although myEDmatch is one of of the best ways to find a teaching job these days, one method continues to work better than any other: word of mouth.


Go to step 1

Step 1: First, make sure your privacy settings are set to anyone. Then, to share it on social media, click the appropriate blue share button(s) located in the top-right corner of your profile.

ExpertTip: When you share your profile on Twitter, include @myEDmatch in your tweet and we'll retweet to share it with all of our school followers!

Step 2: To share your profile in messages or applications, grab your unique URL from a Facebook, Twitter, or LinkedIn post you've already shared, or by clicking on the blue Twitter share button. Then, copy and paste the link into your message.

ExpertTip: If you're a student or otherwise openly active job seeker, include a link to your profile in your email signature. You never know who'll see it and pass it along!
