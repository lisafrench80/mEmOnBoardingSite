In consultation with our Human Resources Advisory Board, comprised of some leading talent recruiters and hiring professionals education, we’ve developed a free suite of comprehensive candidate management tools for you to track applicants through the selection process, score candidates according to the criteria that matter to you, collaborate with teammates on interview notes, and send interview free response questions of your choosing and easily and efficiently as possible.

ExpertTip: Recruiters and principals should feel free to use their own hiring practices and processes, but we offer this suite of free convenience features for you to use at your discretion: these features include being able to send interview questions that you can customize and view them directly from the dashboard, take and share interview notes related to hiring competencies, and being able to track and manage your candidates through the stages of the application process directly within and integrated into our platform to ease the selection process for you.


Go to step 1

Step 1: Use the “Candidate” section of the “Dashboard” to explore your pipeline of candidates by campus and position, or export to Excel or CSV. Click on each candidate’s name to review her profile, portfolio, resume, and Match Score. The candidate list is automatically sorted by Match Score (highest to lowest), but you can easily sort by other fields. You can also filter the list by the Job Position.

Go to step 2

Step 2: Send additional application questions (choose from over 20 standard ones, and the option to enter customer questions) to candidates with ease. Candidates will be notified when interview questions are sent to them, and you will receive notification as soon as that candidate completes the interview questions. All of this information is easily accessible directly from the Candidates section at the bottom of your Dashboard.

Back
Go to step 3

Step 3: Keep notes on and assess candidates based on your selection criteria in the Screening Notes. Click on the clipboard icon to pull up a candidate’s notes, which are shared between all users within your school. Select competencies to assess at each stage of the selection process, score candidates on each one, and add your own ratings/rankings as desired.

Back
Go to step 4

Step 4: Update candidates’ statuses throughout the selection process and close out job postings as you are hiring candidates. Change a candidate’s status by selecting the appropriate stage from the dropdown menu under “Status” on the dashboard.

ExpertTip: You can also change applicant statuses in bulk by clicking “Actions” and then “Change Status.” Please note that candidates receive notification updates when their statuses have been updated, so please be sure that you intend to move an applicant forward in the process or that you intend to reject them before updating their status.


As you change the status of applicants, you can view summaries of where candidates are in the process At-A-Glance from the ribbon at the top of your Dashboard. You can filter by campus and position and see exactly how many candidates are in each stage of your application pipeline.

From the “Jobs” section of the Dashboard, once you have filled a position, change its status to “Closed.” This will ensure that your Jobs list does not get cluttered, and will move Closed jobs to the “Archived” tab in the “Jobs” menu. As a convenience feature, if you want to reuse information from an old job posting, you can easily do so by duplicating that job posting and updating the start date, etc. (See “Create Job Postings”)
